# Marketing Research Skills

This directory contains a collection of specialized skills designed to perform various aspects of marketing research. These skills leverage external data sources and analytical capabilities to provide insights into markets, competitors, and potential opportunities.

## Available Skills:

*   **Audience Segmentation (`audience_segmentation.md`)**:
    Researches and defines target audience segments for a given product or service, helping to understand customer demographics, psychographics, behaviors, and needs.

*   **Competitor Intelligence (`competitor_intelligence.md`)**:
    Gathers and synthesizes information about specific competitors to understand their strategies, products, market positioning, and overall performance.

*   **Market Trend Analysis (`market_trend_analysis.md`)**:
    Identifies and analyzes emerging market trends based on a user-provided query, providing insights into current and future market dynamics.

*   **Opportunity Identification (`opportunity_identification.md`)**:
    Identifies new market niches or product opportunities within a specified industry by researching various market aspects.

Each skill is designed to be invoked independently with specific parameters to perform its designated research function.
