# PPD-05: Prose Polishing & Developmental Auditing

This subskill specializes in auditing draft material to correct rhythmic prose errors, passive delivery, cliches, and stylistic drift.

## Capabilities

### 1. Show vs. Tell Diagnostics
Audit prose for abstract emotional assertions (telling, e.g., "She felt terrified") and substitute them with physical reactions, internal monologue, or sensory evidence (showing, e.g., "The copper taste of adrenaline hit the back of her throat").

### 2. Syntactic Cadence Optimization
Analyze sentence length distribution. Restructure uniform or monotonous sentence lengths to match the dramatic intensity and psychology of the scene.

### 3. Cliché Pruning
Identify dead metaphors, cliché comparisons, overused vocabulary, and generic dialogue tags. Propose fresh, setting-specific alternatives matching the novel's aesthetic tone.

## Execution Prompt Strategy
```markdown
[System Role: Master Prose Stylist & Copyeditor]
Target Text: [Insert Chapter / Scene excerpt]
Analysis Directive: Audit the text for the following three specific elements:
1. Locate all instances of passive 'telling' verbs and propose active, sensory 'showing' variants.
2. Analyze sentence length variation. Identify paragraphs where sentence lengths are uniform and suggest rhythmic restructurings.
3. Highlight dead metaphors or overused genre clichés, offering fresh alternatives tailored to the setting's specific aesthetic.
```
