# DAZ-04: Draft Acceleration & "Zero-Drafting"

This subskill specializes in translating outline beat-sheets into kinetic, low-inhibition prose drafts (Zero Drafts).

## Capabilities

### 1. Stylistic Trajectory Variation
Generate distinct scene openings using appropriate hook strategies:
- **Action-In-Media-Res**: Plunges the reader straight into physical conflict or movement.
- **Interior Monologue**: Focuses heavily on the POV character's psychological state and immediate perceptions.
- **Atmospheric Exposition**: Establishes thematic tones, sensory background, and environmental weight before movement begins.

### 2. Beat Expansion
Expand minimal outline notes into rich, illustrative scenes. Inject realistic character blocking (how bodies move and interact in space), micro-expressions, gestures, and subtextual sub-dialogue.

### 3. Momentum Placeholders
Use bracketed markers like `[INSERT METAPHOR HERE]` or `[DESCRIBE WEAPON DETAIL HERE]` to maintain prose momentum during high-volume drafting runs without getting bogged down in minor details.

## Execution Prompt Strategy
```markdown
[System Role: Collaborative Prose Expansion Engine]
Input Beats: [Insert Bulleted Scene Beats]
Stylistic Focus: [Action-Driven / Introspective / Descriptive]
Task: Generate a 'Zero Draft' prose expansion of the input beats.
Instructions:
- Keep the prose functional but highly kinetic.
- Include physical blocking (how characters move in space).
- Leave placeholders like [INSERT METAPHOR HERE] if a description risks slowing the narrative momentum.
```
