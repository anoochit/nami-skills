# IWB-01: Ideation & World-Building Engine

This subskill specializes in generating non-derivative speculative lore, sensory-rich environments, and subverting standard genre tropes.

## Capabilities

### 1. Trope Subversion Engine
Instead of utilizing standard genre formulas (e.g., *The Chosen One*, *Enemies to Lovers*), apply structured inversions:
- **Ideological Subversion**: Flip the underlying morality or core beliefs of a trope.
- **Structural Failure**: Highlight the logistical or systemic collapse of a trope's premises.
- **Role Reversal**: Swapping dynamic power hierarchies between key players.

### 2. Sensory Matrix Generation
Enforce a balanced, non-visual dominant five-layer sensory profile for settings (aim for 2 specific, non-generic descriptions per category):
- **Sight**: Shifting illumination, dynamic forms, contrasting shapes.
- **Sound**: Low acoustic undercurrents, sudden echoes, resonant ambient vibrations.
- **Smell**: Ecological scents, industrial compounds, natural decay.
- **Texture**: Atmospheric pressure, temperature gradients, surface particulate.
- **Taste**: Suspended moisture, dry ash, copper/metallic mineral dust.

### 3. Lore Consistency Validation
Map environmental logic directly to socioeconomic and ecological structures (e.g., a resource-scarce community must exhibit clear trade infrastructure, supply preservation, and currency systems matching that scarcity).

## Execution Prompt Strategy
```markdown
[System Role: Master Speculative Worldbuilder]
Context: Setting up [Setting Core Idea].
Task: Generate an environmental matrix for [Location Name].
Constraints:
1. Provide a 5-senses sensory profile with 2 entries per sense. Avoid generic adjectives.
2. Outline one major logistical or economic conflict native to this location.
3. Subvert one standard genre trope associated with this type of setting.
```
